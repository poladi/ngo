What We Do

Scientific Cooperation and Integration Center is a non-entrepreneurial (non-commercial) legal entity founded in 2018. Our mission is to promote tolerance and respect for human rights.
The organization aims to support the integration of migrants in the society of Georgia. We are the bridge between immigrants and Georgian society.
We support immigrants, allowing them to integrate into Georgian society and socio-economic reality.
SCIC encourages young people to be actively involved in social life. Our goal is to support young scientists, researchers, to carry out surveys, researches and projects in the field of migration.
