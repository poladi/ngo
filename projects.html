Projects

“Support the integration of migrants in Georgia”

Georgia is multicultural country, situated in South Caucasus. More and more foreigners visit Georgia and desire to settle permanently or temporarily in Georgia. These are many asylum seekers, who for various reasons can’t return to their country and they are looking for a new home.It brings new challenges and needs to support integration between the inhabitants and the migrants from culturally different countries.

  • We provide a series of services that will help foreigners integrate into Georgian society. We offer career counseling, legal counseling and Georgian language classes.
  • To respond to the needs of migrants, we give special information and consultations regarding to the issues of legalization of stay, legal work, integration assistance in the broad sense. The Organization offers Georgian language Courses, different types of workshops to promote Georgian culture and language.
  • Project – Migration Museum
    Project - "Migration Museum in Georgia" is a space for exhibitions, events and workshops based on the stories of migrants, migration history.
