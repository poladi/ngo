Georgian Language Courses

Why you should learn Georgian Language

Georgian is a unique, rich language with a centuries-long history, which has its own ancient script. Georgian alphabet consists of 33 letters, denoting 5 vowels and 28 consonants. Georgian literature is one of the most important part of the World literature. Learning Georgian language as foreign language is important for those who live in Georgia or outside of Georgia. Our center will help immigrant children to learn Georgian language, history and culture. Children will participate in different types of workshops.

We invite you on Georgian Language courses:

  • A1 for Russian-speaking people (basic/beginner)
  • A2 for Russian-speaking people (intermediate)
  • A1 for English-speaking people (beginner)
  • A2 for English-speaking people (intermediate)
